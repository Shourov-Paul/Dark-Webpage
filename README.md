## 🌟 Key Features
- **Ultra-Modern Glassmorphism UI**: A gorgeous floating control panel featuring iOS-inspired sliders, neon glowing interactions, and incredibly smooth fluid animations.
- **Dynamic Dark Mode**: Instantly injects an intelligent dark screen tint over any active webpage. Slide to dynamically adjust the intensity.
- **Eye Protect Mode**: Toggles a warm amber blue-light filtering overlay to protect your eyes during late-night coding or reading sessions. 
- **Integrated Lock-Screen Clock**: A beautiful ambient hero clock that tracks real-time data seamlessly inside the panel.
- **Smart Memory State Persistence**: The extension actively saves its absolute X/Y coordinates, toggle states, and slider intensities directly into Chrome's memory, guaranteeing your layout stays precisely where you left it across tabs and after full page reloads.

## 🚀 Installation Guide

Since the extension is currently locally hosted, you can easily install it deeply into Chrome by following these quick steps:

1. Open Google Chrome.
2. In the URL bar, type in `chrome://extensions/` and press **Enter**.
3. In the top right corner of the Extensions page, toggle the **Developer mode** switch to **ON**.
4. In the top left corner, click the **Load unpacked** button.
5. In the file explorer popup, locate and select this downloaded `chrome extention` folder.
6. The exact **Dark Webpage** extension card will immediately appear. 

## 🖱️ How to Use

1. Click the puzzle-piece icon at the top right of your Chrome browser, and **Pin** the new Dark Webpage sun/moon logo to your permanent active toolbar.
2. Navigate to any website (e.g., `https://google.com` or `https://github.com`).
3. Click the Dark Webpage logo in your toolbar to instantly spawn the floating glass control panel!
4. **Drag** the header to safely move it anywhere on your screen.
5. **Toggle** the Dark Mode or Eye Protect buttons and interact with the premium sliding glass tracks to set your exact preferred intensity!
